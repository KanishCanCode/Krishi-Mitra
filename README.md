## KrishiMitra: Blockchain-based Micro Loan Management System for Farmers

## Overview:

* KrishiMitra is a decentralized platform designed to simplify and secure the process of providing micro-loans to farmers.
* By leveraging blockchain technology, the system ensures transparency, immutability and trust between farmers, banks and lending institutions.
* The goal of KrishiMitra is to reduce exploitation, eliminate middlemen and provide farmers with easier access to credit facilities.

## Key Features

* Blockchain Integration – Ensures secure and tamper-proof transactions.
* Farmer Registration and Verification – Farmers can register and verify their identity on the blockchain.
* Loan Request and Approval – Farmers can request loans, and institutions can approve them transparently.
* Smart Contracts – Automates loan disbursement and repayment tracking.
* Transaction History – View all transactions and approvals in a transparent ledger.

## Tech Stack

* Frontend: ReactJS
* Backend: NodeJS,Express
* Blockchain:Tenderly(Virtual Blockchain), Solidity
* Database: Postgres
* Tools and Libraries: EthersJS

## Smart Contract Functionalities

* Register Farmer / Lender
* Request Loan (by Farmer)
* Approve Loan (by Bank or Lender)
* Repay Loan (through Smart Contract)
* View Transaction Records (Public Ledger)

## Objectives

* Provide transparent and fair loan processing for farmers.
* Reduce the dependency on middlemen.
* Use smart contracts to automate and secure loan workflows.
* Promote financial inclusion through decentralized technology.

## Future Scope

* Integration with government agricultural databases to verify farmer details automatically.
* Implementation of a credit scoring mechanism based on repayment history.
* Addition of mobile app support for better accessibility in rural areas.
* Integration with real banking APIs for direct fund transfer.
* Use of AI-based analytics to assess loan risk and predict defaults.
* Expansion to support multiple blockchains for better scalability and interoperability.
* Inclusion of regional language support to reach a larger farmer base.
